EDDYLEE GH

Edward Leon Khoury popularly known as Eddylee gh is a musician who doubles as a sound engineer | video Director. Eddylee is known all over the world for his creativity and energetic performance anytime he gets on stage. He has to his credit over 500 recorded tunes,produced over 1000 instrumentals, 2 Ep albums, 1 Lp album, 12 music videos of his own, directed over 20 music videos and he is the current artiste of the year for upper west music awards.
