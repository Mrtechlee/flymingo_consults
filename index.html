WHO ARE WE?


Flymingo Consults Ltd. started as a recording studio known as fire flames multimedia in Tumu a town in the Upper West Region of Ghana. Ever since we commenced business in 2011, after five years of opperation it was moved to the Upper West Regional capital Wa in 2015. we have earned ourselves a track record of successful event organisations and training of individuals in sound engineering and photography in the entertainment industry. We pride ourselves in having a strong rapport with our clients and suppliers, a dedicated and experienced management team and an experienced crew with a commitment to continuous improvement. Flymingo Consults embodies a professional, highly skilled and experienced team with over 10 years of combined industry experience. This team has executed projects of various sizes from all over Ghana. With some of the most intelligent minds on our team; giving us the depth and indigenous ‘on the job’ experience, we always come up on top of our game. As of this writing, our team is 10-member strong and growing by the week.


We major in Photography and Videography, Events planning and organisation, website develoment, apps and software development, General painting and decoration and fixing of Raising damp.
